About Me

I'm a game developer with over three years of self-driven programming experience across multiple languages and platforms. I started with Lua in the Source Engine, then expanded into Python, HTML, and Java as I took on larger and more complex projects.

Along the way, I developed various games in Python, built websites, mainly for multiplayer server hosts, and created a few games in Java inspired by cinematic choice-based games. Each project helped me grow stronger in gameplay systems, UI design, storytelling, and seeing projects through from start to finish.

Today, I focus on Roblox development using Lua. I am currently working on two projects, one with a full development team and another as a solo passion project. I am comfortable collaborating with others, taking feedback, and also owning features and systems independently.

I am actively looking for opportunities in the gaming industry where I can apply my skills, continue learning, and contribute to meaningful interactive experiences.
