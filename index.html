The Rat Cave: Reimagined

After the tragic events of 1979, Freddy Fazbear's Pizza was forced to shut its doors, and Fazbear Entertainment collapsed under the weight of scandal. But in 1981, founder Joseph Kane returned with a new vision ‚Äî The Rat Cave. Partnering with Zack Robotics, he brought The Rat Gang to life in a brand-new restaurant built in Chicago.

Over a decade later, in 1993, Stacy Green takes on a simple night shift job at The Rat Cave. What should have been easy money soon turns into something much darker, as Stacy begins to uncover secrets that were never meant to be found.

This isn't just another night trapped in an office, staring at monitors and waiting for animatronics to lunge. This time, you're free to move ‚Äî explore the halls, check the rooms, and wander the restaurant at your own risk. Armed with a flashlight and the laptop you carry from your office, you'll have to keep your wits sharp if you wish to survive until morning.

Technical Details

This game is powered by Pygame and written entirely in Python, rather than the standard Clickteam Fusion engine commonly used for FNaF fan-games.

System Requirements: Windows OS (excluding Windows XP), 8GB+ RAM, Display > 720p, 2GB+ free space
